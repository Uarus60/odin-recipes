Ribeye Steak

Home

Finished product of Ribeye

To get a steak similar to this we will be cooking our steak in a grill

Ingredients

  • Steak Ribeye cut
  • Seasoning of your choosing

Steps

  1. Bring steak to room temperature.
  2. Heat grill to medium heat.
  3. Season steaks and grill it between 5 to 7 minutes each side, depending on the desired done-ness.
  4. Remove steaks
  5. Dot it with butter, and allow them to rest for about 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
