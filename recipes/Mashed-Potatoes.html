Finished Product of Mashed Potatoes

Today I will be describing how to make a bowl of mashed potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of naking potaoes,peeled and quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Salt and ground black pepper (added to taste)

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients.
  2. bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and garlic, lower heat to medium, and simer until potatoes are tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. When the potatoes are almost finished, heat milk and butter in a small saucepan over low heat untill butter is melted.
  4. Drain potatoes and return to the pot. Slowly add warm milk mixture, blending it in with a potato masher or electric mixer untill potatoes are smooth and creamy
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve and enjoy
