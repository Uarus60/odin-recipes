Today I will be showing you how to make Grilled Asparagus.

Ingredients

  • Asparagus
  • Olive oil
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Prep and sesason the asparagus.
  2. Pre-Heat the grill to medium heat and brush the grill grates clean.
  3. Place the asparagus across the grillo grates perpendicular to the bars.
  4. Grill until crisp-tender
